You can keep writing in Obsidian and treat this repo as your publish target.

Suggested structure in your vault:

- Working notes anywhere you like
- Final blog drafts synced or copied into `content/posts`

Each post uses frontmatter for metadata (`title`, `date`, optional `draft`).
